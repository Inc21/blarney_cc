About Blarney CC

Established in 1886, Blarney Cycling Club is one of Ireland's oldest cycling clubs. We are primarily a road racing club with multiple national champions among our ranks. We have riders from A1 through A4 and LC on our team.


If you wish to partake in a spin or If you want to move from LC to A1, hit us up and join the club.


Please contact us here through the contact form or send a PM on our Facebook page.
